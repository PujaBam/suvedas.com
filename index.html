Our selection process

Suvedas reviews and screens a large number of investment proposals, conducts thorough due
diligence, and negotiates and finalizes the terms of successful investments. Suvedas also provides
ongoing support to its portfolio companies to help them grow and succeed

Review and screen potential investments:

Suvedas reviews and screens a large number of investment proposals to identify those that align with its criteria and fit its portfolio

Conduct due diligence:

If a startup passes the initial review, Suvedas conducts a thorough due diligence process to evaluate the potential of the investment.

Negotiate and finalize the investment:

If Suvedas decides to move forward with an investment, it negotiates and finalizes the terms with the startup.

Monitor and support the startup:

Suvedas works closely with its portfolio companies to support their growth and development.
